Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This privacy policy explains how Morse Beacon (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our Android application.

Data you enter

Text you type for Morse transmission is processed locally on your device. It is not transmitted to our servers or any third party. Message text is not stored permanently unless you leave it in the input field during your session.

Camera flash

The app uses your device's camera flash (torch/LED) to output Morse code signals. The app does not access the camera preview, capture photos, or record video. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

Settings and learn progress

App preferences (speed, timing options, and display settings) and learn-mode statistics (quiz scores, streaks, and mastered characters) are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

No data collection

The app does not collect, transmit, or share personal information. It does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Core functionality works fully offline without an internet connection.

Internet access

The app may open external links (such as this privacy policy or support email) only when you choose to tap those links. No background network requests are made.

Purchases

If you purchase the app on Google Play, payment is processed by Google. We do not receive your payment card details. Google may collect purchase-related information according to its own privacy policy.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support.morsebeacon@gmail.com.
