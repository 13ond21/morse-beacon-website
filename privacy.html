Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 July 2026

This privacy policy explains how Morse Beacon (“the app”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our Android application published under LuckyTools / package com.luckytools.morse_beacon.

Data you enter

Text you type for Morse transmission is processed locally on your device. We do not operate a server that receives your messages. Message text is not uploaded to us. Premium users may keep recent messages in local history on the device only.

Camera flash

The app uses your device's camera flash (torch/LED) to output Morse code signals. The app does not access the camera preview, capture photos, or record video. No images or video are stored or transmitted by Morse Beacon.

Settings and learn progress

Preferences (speed, timing, display options), learn-mode statistics, custom phrases (Premium), and local history are stored with on-device storage (SharedPreferences). This information is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising (free tier only)

The free version shows ads provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC): banner ads and full-screen interstitial ads (approximately every 90 seconds while the app is idle — never while the LED is actively transmitting Morse).

Google and its partners may collect and process data such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and ad interaction data to deliver and measure ads, subject to Google’s policies. See Google Privacy Policy and AdMob & privacy.

Premium (one-time in-app purchase) removes all ads. When Premium is active, the app does not load AdMob ad units for that session.

Purchases

Premium unlocks are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your payment card details. Google may process purchase-related information under its own privacy policy. Purchase status may be stored locally on your device to keep Premium unlocked and may be revalidated with Google Play when you restore purchases.

Internet access

Free tier requires network access for ads. The app may also open external links (this policy, terms, support email) when you choose to tap them. Core Morse flash and learn features work offline once the app is installed; ads and store purchases require connectivity.

Analytics

Morse Beacon does not include a separate analytics product (for example, we do not ship Firebase Analytics for usage tracking of your Morse content). Advertising partners may collect their own measurement data as described above on the free tier.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The free tier contains ads that are not intended for a child-directed experience under Families policies.

Your choices

  • Use the free tier with ads, or purchase Premium to remove ads
  • Adjust device advertising preferences / reset advertising ID in Android settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove local settings, history, and learn progress

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect any changes.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support.morsebeacon@gmail.com.
